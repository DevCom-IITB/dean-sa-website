Functioning

The mandate of the Office of the Dean of Student Affairs is to coordinate and regulate all the above aspects of student life at IIT Bombay. This is carried out primarily through two bodies :

  1. The Hostels Coordinating Unit ( HCU )
  2. The Students Gymkhana
The HCU is under the charge of the Chairman, HCU. He coordinates and interfaces with the Council of Wardens. All of these functionaries are members of the faculty of IIT Bombay. Day-to-day matters in the hostels are looked after by the Hall Manager and by an elected Hostel Council of students led by the Hostel General Secretary.

Students Gymkhana has a group of highly qualified Sports Officers and other instructors to run all the activities. Coordination to these is provided by two faculty members officiating as Chairman, Sports, and Chairman, Cultural. A key role is played in coordinating the activities and in representing the students by an apex group of elected General Secretaries.
